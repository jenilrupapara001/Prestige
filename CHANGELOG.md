- Editorial-grade design system for fashion brands
- Mobile-optimized responsive layouts
- Advanced product display options (grid, carousel, shop-the-look)
- Color swatch support for product variants
- Product quick view functionality
- Related products section with manual and automatic selection
- Recently viewed products tracking
- Modular content sections for flexible page building
- Image with text overlay sections
- Video background support
- Slideshow section for hero content
- Timeline section for brand storytelling
- Testimonials section
- Instagram feed integration
- Newsletter signup forms
